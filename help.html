Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session with a skill partner? expand_more
To book a session, browse our skill categories or search for a specific talent. Once you find a partner, click on their profile and select "Request Swap". Choose a time from their available slots and send a short message about what you'd like to learn.
What if a user doesn't show up to our scheduled meeting? expand_more
If a user misses a session without 24-hour notice, you can report a "No-Show" via the session history page. We value everyone's time, and repeated no-shows may lead to account suspension. Credits are usually refunded automatically in these cases.
Is skill swapping completely free? expand_more
Skill Swap operates on a credit system. You earn credits by teaching others, which you can then use to learn from others. While the exchange of knowledge is free of monetary cost, we offer a premium tier for those who want extra credits without teaching or advanced matchmaking features.
Can I teach multiple skills? expand_more
Absolutely! You can list as many skills as you are comfortable teaching. Simply add them to your profile and specify your expertise level for each.
